🏆 Path to Victory — Title Odds

Multiplying each seed's matchup probability through all 3 rounds gives their overall chance of winning the tournament.

    How it works

    1. Seeding: athletes are ranked 1–16 by personal best time (fastest = #1). Seeds are distributed across 4 groups using a snake pattern (1‑8‑9‑16, 2‑7‑10‑15, …) so that each group has a balanced mix of strong and weak athletes.
    2. Probability: for any matchup we compute how much percent faster the favorite's PB is, then convert that to a win probability. The formula is clamped between 50% and 99% — in real life even heavy favorites lose sometimes!
    3. Path to Victory: we trace each athlete's most-likely opponents through every round and multiply their matchup probabilities to get their overall tournament title odds.
